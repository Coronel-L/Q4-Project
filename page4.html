Born Laufey Lin Bing Jónsdóttir, Laufey [Lay-Vay] is an Icelandic-Chinese singer-songwriter that brings a jazz-influenced, traditional pop style into the 21st century with calming songs that are inspired by artists like Peggy Lee, Ella Fitzgerald, and Billie Holiday. She made her debut in 2020 with her viral song “Street by Street,” which was popular on several social media platforms and was recognized by artists such as Billie Eilish.

Laufey released her debut EP, Typical of Me, in April of 2021 and later expanded to a full-length album in 2022, Everything I Know About Love, which was anchored by the bossa nova-inspired single “Falling Behind.” This album contained multiple hits such as Valentine and Falling Behind and eventually reached the Top 20 in her native Iceland. In the following month, she embarked on her North American tour, and her concert LP A Night at the Symphony Orchestra with the Iceland Symphony Orchestra came out in 2023.

In 2023, she also released her sophomore album, Bewitched, which brought her wider international acclaim, including a Top 30 placement on the Billboard 200 and the Grammy Award for Best Traditional Pop Vocal Album.
